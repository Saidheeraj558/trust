About Us

Orphanage Trust is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing a brighter future for orphaned children. Our mission is to create a safe and loving environment where these children can thrive and grow. With a focus on education, healthcare, and emotional support, we aim to empower every child to reach their full potential.

Founded in 2005, we have been serving our community for over a decade. Our dedicated team of volunteers and professionals work tirelessly to ensure that every child in our care receives the love and care they deserve.

Our Services

Orphanage Home

We provide shelter along with foodto orphans, street children and make them be a part of our family.

Education

We take care of all education needs of our children and make them well educated citizens of this Country.

Day Care

We provide day care services for orphans and street children and provide them with necessary support.

Skill Development

We provide skill development programs for orphan children and adults so that they can be self dependent.
